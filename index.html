Off‑road motorcycling on film — from dirt to dust

Dirt2Dust is an Australian video production outfit founded in 2008 by Adrian Bruce with Darrell Greig, capturing the grit, speed, and spirit of off‑road motorcycling across rallies, enduro, and motocross.

About and history

Born in Australia’s off‑road scene, Dirt2Dust focused on authentic rider‑eye storytelling: helmet cams in the trees, roost in the lens, and long days on course to bring audiences inside the tape.

  • 2008 launch: Company founded by Adrian Bruce with Darrell Greig; early work centered on enduro and trail events.
  • ADB collaboration: A Dirt2Dust film was distributed nationally as a cover‑mounted DVD with Australasian Dirt Bike (ADB) magazine in 2008.
  • Event footprint: Coverage across motocross and enduro; sponsorship at the Sunny Corner Trail Bike Rally (2008) with inclusion on official sponsor lists and third‑party event write‑ups.
